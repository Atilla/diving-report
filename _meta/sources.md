# Sources

This report synthesizes data from the following categories. Individual destination files cite specific sources in their Status, Conservation, and Trip Reports sections.

## Scientific & Monitoring

- NOAA Coral Reef Watch — real-time sea surface temperature (SST), Degree Heating Week (DHW) alerts, bleaching forecasts (coralreefwatch.noaa.gov)
- ICRI/SHAMS alerts — International Coral Reef Initiative status updates
- GCRMN Caribbean Report 2025 — Global Coral Reef Monitoring Network regional assessment
- CORDIO East Africa — Western Indian Ocean bleaching reports

## Peer-Reviewed Literature

- PeerJ, MDPI, Springer

## Diver Reports & Publications

- ScubaBoard trip reports and forum threads (2024-2026)
- Dive operator field reports
- Mongabay — environmental journalism
- Various diving publications and magazines

### World Adventure Divers (worldadventuredivers.com)

Dive travel blog by Florine, running since 2012. Firsthand trip reports with dated dive logs, specific parameters (depth, temp, visibility), and detailed site descriptions. Covers Indonesia, Caribbean, Eastern Pacific, Mediterranean, and French Polynesia destinations. Budget/independent travel perspective. Used for 2022–2025 trip report data.

## German-Language Sources

Added April 2026 cross-reference pass. Primary German diving community sources used for 2023–2026 data:

- **taucher.net** — Germany's largest diving forum (300k+ posts). Key threads: Red Sea bleaching (ioz94550, ioz94650, ioz95875, ioz95440, ioz95771), Maldives 2025 house reefs (ioz95572), global reef state (ioz95165). Extensive first-hand documentation of 2023–2024 Egyptian Red Sea bleaching events.
- **scuba-people.com** — multilingual community, German section

### Magazines & Publications

- **tauchen.de / TAUCHEN Magazin** — Europe's primary diving magazine (since 1978). Key articles: Raja Ampat (Dec 2023, Feb 2025), Halmahera (Sep 2024), Maldives multi-resort (Nov 2024), Spirit of Maldives liveaboard (Jan 2026), Elphinstone (Aug 2024), Egypt crisis overview (Jul 2025), GBR/Ningaloo simultaneous bleaching (Mar 2025), Philippines (Dec 2025), Galapagos (Sep 2024).
- **tauchmagazin.com** — "Tropische Korallenriffe am Kipppunkt" (global tipping point overview, 2025) — >80% of reefs in 80+ countries affected by fourth mass bleaching event.

### Blogs

- **Scuba Travel Scandinavia (scubatravelscandinavia.de)** — operator blog with destination reports. Key posts: Alor (Oct 2025, Jun–Jul visit), Raja Ampat/Misool (2026, ILIKE liveaboard), Cozumel/Playa del Carmen (Autumn 2025).
- **Helmut Stauch blog (helmutstauchblog.blogspot.com)** — TSC Poseidon Munich club diver. Key posts: Ring of Fire/Banda Sea (Sep–Oct 2024), Socorro (Apr 2023), French Polynesia (Oct 2022).
- **Aqua Active Reiseblog (aquaactive.de/reiseblog)** — operator blog. Key posts: Halmahera/Sali Bay (2026), Maldives liveaboard (2026).
- **Der Taucherblog (dertaucherblog.de)** — Red Sea destination overviews and sustainable tourism articles (2024–2025).

### German Science Journalism

- **scinexx.de** — "Korallenbleiche reicht überraschend tief" (deep bleaching at 60–90m, Seychelles/Mauritius/Tanzania, 2024–2025).
- **divediscover.de** — "Korallen im Roten Meer 2026: Ägyptens Riffe unter Druck" — quantitative Red Sea bleaching data (Wadi El Gemal 50.1%, Hurghada 9%, coral growth rate collapse from >2cm/year to <0.5cm/year).

## French-Language Sources

Added April 2026. Primary French diving and monitoring sources used for Mauritius, Réunion, and Seychelles data.

### Scientific & Monitoring

- **IFRECOR** (ifrecor.fr) — French Indian Ocean Reef Network. Monitors reef health across French overseas territories (Réunion, Mayotte, Scattered Islands, Martinique, Guadeloupe). Key document: "Blanchissement corallien dans les îles de l'océan indien en 2024" — confirms Réunion spared in 2024, severely impacted in 2025; Mauritius relatively less affected in 2024.
- **UTOPIAN project** (2021–2026) — French-funded long-term coral monitoring at Réunion. Source of 18% hard coral cover baseline figure (pre-2025).

### Regional News & Environmental Journalism

- **La1ère / France 3 Réunion** (la1ere.franceinfo.fr/reunion) — French public broadcaster's Réunion service. Key 2025 article: "Le blanchissement 'intense' du récif corallien de La Réunion, aggravé par le réchauffement et le cyclone Garance" — quantified 2025 bleaching impact and Cyclone Garance damage.
- **Reporterre** (reporterre.net) — French environmental journalism. Key article: "Le cyclone Garance, fossoyeur des coraux de La Réunion" (March 2025) — documented ~100ha damaged, ~40ha destroyed, Saint-Leu >90% coral mortality.
- **Vert.eco** — French environmental reporting. Quantified €49M annual reef service value for Réunion; documented ≥80% bleaching extent and projected ~10% surviving cover post-2025.
- **Imazpress** (imazpress.com) — Réunion local news outlet. Granular 2025 coverage of bleaching progression, Cyclone Garance aftermath, and dive center closures.

### Magazines & Diving Publications

- **SUBAQUA Magazine / FFESSM** (subaqua.ffessm.fr) — official publication of the French diving federation. N°317 (Nov–Dec 2024): 6-page portfolio on Mauritius marine life ("Rencontre avec le Sublime"). Updated article (Sept 2024): "Les Seychelles entre granit et corail" — granite dive conditions at inner islands; Aldebaran wreck ("Seychelles: un jardin d'Eden"). Réunion: SSI/FFESSM certification crossover programs documented at named operators.

### Dive Travel Blogs & Agencies

- **Dive Trip Adékua blog** (plongee.voyages-adekua.fr) — French diving travel agency. 2025 blog post "Le meilleur des Seychelles… mais en mieux!" reports Amirantes now accessible without military escort via private catamaran.
- **ZeSea Blog** (zesea.com) — by Anthony Leydet (underwater photographer). Articles on Mauritius NW coast and Rodrigues; Nov 2022 assessment of Mauritius reef quality.
- **Sous-Les-Étoiles** (souslesetoiles974.re) — Réunion diving guide (2025 edition). Named operators, site descriptions, and conditions overview.
- **Ultramarina** (ultramarina.com) — oldest French diving travel agency (founded 1986). Destination coverage for Mauritius, Réunion, and Seychelles.

### Forums

- **Plongeur.com** (plongeur.com) — France's primary diving forum. Active threads on Réunion/Mauritius conditions; documented hammerhead sightings at Fosse aux Requins and Whale Rock (Mauritius).
